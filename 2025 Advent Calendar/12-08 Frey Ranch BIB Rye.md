**Notes:**

This one is **a lot less minty and dill-flavored** than many ryes. The **initial heat** on the sip is real, but the **finish is great** and keeps you coming back. Overall, it's **balanced and complex**. **Top notch** despite the initial burn.

**Frey Ranch Bottled-In-Bond Straight Rye Whiskey** - **100% Winter Cereal Rye** mash bill (all grains grown on site). Aged for a **minimum of five years** and bottled at **100 proof**.

- $57 at Total Wine.
- Distillery notes its commitment to farming with **crop rotation** and **zero electricity irrigation**.
- It's a Double Gold medal winner from the **San Francisco World Spirit Competition**.
- The distiller says they wanted to "showcase the quality of our slow-grown Rye grains without the impact of any other flavors."
